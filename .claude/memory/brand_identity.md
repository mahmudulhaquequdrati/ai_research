## Brand Voice
- **Confident** — We know our stuff, no hedging
- **Technical but accessible** — Deep expertise, clear communication
- **Direct** — No fluff, no filler, lead with value
- **Results-oriented** — Always tie to outcomes and ROI
- **Human** — Personality welcome, corporate speak banned

**Why:** In the AI space, most companies sound identical — generic, hype-driven, buzzword-heavy. Our brand cuts through by being specific, honest, and results-focused.

**How to apply:** Every piece of content, every client communication, every social post must pass the "would a smart human say this?" test. If it sounds like it was generated by AI without editing, rewrite it.

## Brand Values
1. **Execution over theory** — We ship, not just plan
2. **Quality over quantity** — One great solution beats ten mediocre ones
3. **Transparency** — Honest about capabilities and limitations
4. **Continuous improvement** — Always getting better (autoresearch mindset)
5. **Client success = our success** — Aligned incentives
