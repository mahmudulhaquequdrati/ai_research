## Company: AI Skills

### Revenue Streams
1. **Custom Software Development** — Building solutions for clients
2. **AI Integration Services** — Helping businesses adopt AI tools and workflows
3. **Automation Consulting** — Process automation for operational efficiency
4. **Digital Products** — SaaS tools, templates, courses, and digital assets
5. **Marketing Services** — Content, social media, and brand building for select clients

**Why:** Building multiple revenue streams for resilience and scale. Software and AI services are high-margin, products provide recurring revenue, marketing builds the brand that sells everything else.

**How to apply:** Every technical decision should consider which revenue stream it serves. Prefer solutions that can be productized (built once, sold many times) over pure services (sold once, built each time).

### Target Markets
- SMBs wanting AI-powered efficiency
- Enterprises needing custom AI solutions
- Startups needing technical co-founder level support
- Digital-first businesses needing automation

### Competitive Advantage
- We build AND use AI — practitioners, not just vendors
- Speed of delivery through our own automation
- Full-stack capability (strategy to deployment)
- Results-focused (we measure ROI, not activity)
