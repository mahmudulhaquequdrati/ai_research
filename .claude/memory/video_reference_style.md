## Reference: Diandra Escobar Style (YouTube)

**Video**: "This AI Agent Gets Me LinkedIn Content Before I Wake Up"
**Creator**: Diandra Escobar (@diandraescobarr) — Colombia's #1 content strategist (Favikon), founder of Distinctiva Strategies
**URL**: https://youtu.be/KlawrDJuqYc

### Editing Philosophy (from Diandra's own words)

1. **Pattern interrupts** — "Top creators don't give their audience a chance to get bored by leveraging pattern interrupts" — sound effects, music changes, camera angle switches, surprise graphics
2. **Peaks and valleys** — "A great edit will take the audience on a journey by strategically restricting and revealing information, will have peaks and valleys in the dynamics, action, and pacing"
3. **Emotionally-driven cuts** — "Editing is about making creative choices in the context of a story. A lot of these decisions aren't actually logical but driven by what will make the audience feel something"
4. **Substance over spectacle** — Real systems, battle-tested. No AI hype. The editing serves the content, not the other way around
5. **Professional agency quality** — She charges $4,000-$6,500+/month for content services. Her YouTube is portfolio-level production

### Content Format

- **Type**: Educational walkthrough / practical tutorial
- **Duration**: 10-20 minutes (long-form)
- **Structure**: Hook → Problem → Solution Preview → Step-by-Step Walkthrough → Results → CTA
- **Tone**: Direct, no-hype, operator-first, anti-clickbait
- **Audience**: B2B marketers, founders, content creators

### Visual Style

- Talking head + screen recording hybrid
- Picture-in-picture (webcam over screen recording)
- Clean graphics: lower thirds, step indicators, stat callouts
- Data overlays and flowcharts
- Screenshots of real results (LinkedIn posts, analytics)
- Professional color grading (bright, clean)
- Styled captions/subtitles

### Audio Style

- Clear voiceover (primary)
- Background music at low volume (upbeat tech)
- Sound effects for pattern interrupts (whoosh, pop, impact)
- Music energy shifts to match content pacing

### Production Quality Bar

- 1080p minimum, clean lighting
- Professional audio (no room echo, no background noise)
- Consistent color grade across all footage
- Captions on all content
- Thumbnail: face + text + contrast

**How to apply:** Every video produced by AI Skills should target this quality level. Use as the reference standard for script structure, editing decisions, and production quality.
